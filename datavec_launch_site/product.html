Why it matters

DataVec attacks computational waste at the execution layer.

The company is not selling a prettier dashboard. The product is aimed at the expensive parts of cloud execution: runtime setup, memory movement, cold starts, and coordination overhead.

A

Developer productivity stays

Teams keep high level development speed instead of rewriting everything in low level languages.

B

Runtime waste drops

Workloads are prepared and run in a way that is designed to use less CPU, memory, and startup time.

Core themes

The platform is built around five ideas.

Locality

Keep computation close to the state and data it needs.

Residency

Keep workloads warm instead of rebuilding them repeatedly.

Optimization

Move expensive work to build time wherever possible.

Observability

Show teams what changed in latency, memory, CPU use, and cost.

Spend control

Use alerts, budgets, and hard caps so teams can scale without surprise invoices.
